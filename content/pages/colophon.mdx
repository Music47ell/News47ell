News47ell, pronounced /njuːz for hɛl/ is a tech news website started back in 2013 that also serves as the place for experiments conducted by me, Ahmet ALMAZ.

### Who's Ahmet ALMAZ?

I'm a Full-Stack Developer from the city of Kahramanmaraş in Türkiye. I'm a self-taught developer who started his journey back in 2013. I'm a huge fan of open-source software and I try to contribute to the community as much as I can.

If you're interested in working with me, you can find my resume [here](/resume).

Drizzle ORM, MDX, Next.js, PNPM, Tailwind CSS, Turso, TypeScript, Vercel
